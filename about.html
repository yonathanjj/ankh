Who We Are

Ankh is a dynamic and innovative company founded by young entrepreneurs with a shared passion for art, science, and technology. Since 2014 E.C., we have dedicated ourselves to transforming Ethiopia's rich cultural knowledge into impactful services, groundbreaking films, and a new generation of educational content that inspires and empowers.

Mission

To empower our community through transformative creative and scientific education, while providing a suite of innovative services that are meticulously designed to inspire profound growth, lasting development, and authentic self-expression in every individual we reach.

Vision

To become a leading global creative and educational force, celebrated for transforming lives and communities by masterfully blending the depth of Ethiopian culture with the power of modern knowledge through immersive film, world-class training, and cutting-edge technology.

Our Story

Ankh was established purely with the intention of living a purpose and serving the community with an inner drive for quality service. We have since evolved into a vibrant hub for learning, creativity, and impactful storytelling. Our primary focus lies in building and sustaining dynamic platforms that actively nurture personal growth, raw talent, and expansive knowledge across a multitude of creative industries.
